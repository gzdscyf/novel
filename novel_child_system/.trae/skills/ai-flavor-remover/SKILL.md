# AI Flavor Remover

This skill removes AI-generated patterns and makes novel content more natural and human-like.

## When to Use
- After novel-main-writer generates initial content
- When text feels too artificial or formulaic
- When dialogue lacks natural flow
- When descriptions feel generic

## Key Tasks
1. Remove repetitive sentence structures
2. Eliminate generic AI phrases (e.g., "a sense of", "a feeling of")
3. Make dialogue more natural and character-specific
4. Add sensory details and vivid imagery
5. Remove overly formal or academic language
6. Add colloquialisms and informal expressions where appropriate
7. Vary sentence length and structure
8. Remove clichés and overused metaphors

## Focus Areas
- Dialogue: Make it sound like real people talking
- Descriptions: Show, don't tell
- Pacing: Adjust to create natural flow
- Word choice: Use more specific, evocative language
- Emotional resonance: Make feelings more authentic

## Output Requirements
- Maintain the original plot and character consistency
- Keep the same word count (2500 words per chapter)
- Preserve all plot points and foreshadowing
- Ensure no OOC behavior
